Time line of Abraham Lincoln's life

  • 1809: Born in Kentucky
  • 1832: Enlists in a local militia (in Illinois)
  • 1834: Elected to the Illinois General Assembly
  • 1836: Re-elected to his seat in the state legislature
  • 1838: Re-elected for a third term in the Illinois General Assembly
  • 1840: Re-elected for a fourth term in the Illinois General Assembly
  • 1846: Elected to a seat in the United States House of Representativese
  • 1856: Re-elected to the Illinois legislature
  • 1860: Elected the 16th President of the United States
  • 1861: The Civil War begins
  • 1862: Issues preliminary Emancipation Proclamation
  • 1863: Issues final Emancipation Proclamation and deliveres Gettysburg Address at the dedication of Gettysburg National Cemetery
  • 1864: Re-elected to the Presidency
  • 1865: Shot in Ford's Theatre on April 14 and dies the day after
